Meet the Doctor

Dr. Pinnacle, D.C., CCSP

Dr. Pinnacle is a Certified Chiropractic Sports Physician with deep roots in Macomb County and a practice built on the belief that you deserve more than a quick adjustment and a handshake.

After earning his Doctor of Chiropractic degree, Dr. Pinnacle pursued post-doctoral board certification through the American Chiropractic Board of Sports Physicians — becoming a Certified Chiropractic Sports Physician (CCSP). The credential reflects additional training in sports medicine, orthopedic evaluation, rehabilitation, and emergency procedures that goes well beyond standard chiropractic education.

He is also certified in the Webster Technique through the International Chiropractic Pediatric Association (ICPA), allowing him to provide specialized prenatal chiropractic care for expecting mothers across all trimesters.

Pinnacle Chiropractic opened in Clinton Township as an intentional choice. This is home. Dr. Pinnacle grew up in Macomb County, trained here, and built his practice here — not as a franchise location or a corporate clinic, but as a community practice where patients are known by name and care is genuinely personal.

His clinical philosophy is straightforward: find the cause, fix the cause, teach the patient how to stay fixed. Every plan starts with a thorough evaluation — including a detailed history, orthopedic and neurological testing, postural analysis, and imaging when indicated. Every patient leaves the first visit with a clear picture of what's driving their pain and a real plan to address it.

"I built this practice because I believe people in this community deserve a provider who actually listens, evaluates carefully, and gives them a plan — not just a bill." — Dr. Pinnacle

Outside the clinic, Dr. Pinnacle is active in the local athletic community and provides chiropractic coverage at events and competitions throughout Macomb County. He is licensed in both Michigan and Ohio, with a second location in development.

Clinical Philosophy

How we approach care.

01

Find the cause — not just the pain

Pain is a signal, not a diagnosis. Every patient at Pinnacle gets a thorough evaluation before treatment begins. We use orthopedic and neurological testing, postural analysis, and digital X-rays when indicated to identify what's actually driving your symptoms — not just where it hurts.

02

Explain it clearly

You should leave every visit understanding what was found, what was done, and why. Medical jargon that leaves patients confused and dependent isn't good care — it's gatekeeping. We show you your X-rays, explain your findings in plain language, and make sure you're an active participant in your own recovery.

03

Build a plan that ends

The goal of chiropractic care isn't lifetime dependency. It's to resolve the problem, restore function, and give you the tools — the exercises, the movement habits, the awareness — to maintain your results. Some patients choose ongoing wellness care; that's a valid choice. But it should be a choice, not an expectation.

04

Treat the whole person

A shoulder injury in a competitive athlete requires a different approach than the same injury in a 60-year-old office worker. Context matters — your age, activity level, occupation, goals, and history all shape the care plan. We ask enough questions to understand yours.
