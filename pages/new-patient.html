Your First Visit

What happens when you come in.

Your first appointment is about 60 minutes. Here's a clear walkthrough of what that time looks like.

01

Check in & intake

Arrive 10 minutes early to complete your intake paperwork. You can also download and complete it beforehand — we'll email it to you when you book. We'll review your health history, current medications, and the specific concerns that brought you in.

02

Consultation

A focused one-on-one conversation with Dr. Pinnacle about your symptoms, history, and goals. We want to understand not just where it hurts, but how long it's been there, what makes it better or worse, and what you're trying to get back to doing.

03

Examination

A thorough physical examination including postural analysis, range of motion testing, orthopedic and neurological screening, and palpation of the spine and relevant structures. We examine what the history tells us to look at — not a generic checklist.

04

X-rays (if indicated)

Digital X-rays are taken on-site when clinically appropriate — not as a default for every patient. If your history or exam findings suggest imaging would provide meaningful clinical information, we'll take them and review them with you during the same visit.

05

Report of findings

We sit down and explain exactly what we found, what it means, and what we recommend. You'll see your X-rays if taken, hear a clear explanation of your diagnosis, and understand the proposed care plan — including expected timeline, visit frequency, and realistic outcomes.

06

First treatment (same day)

In most cases we begin treatment on the first visit — chiropractic adjustment, soft tissue therapy, and any other indicated procedures. You'll leave having already started the process, not just scheduled for next time.

What to bring

  • Photo ID
  • Insurance card(s)
  • List of current medications
  • Any relevant imaging (MRI, X-ray reports)
  • Referral or prescription if required by your plan
  • Completed intake forms (if downloaded ahead of time)

What to wear

  • Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing you can move in
  • Athletic wear is ideal
  • Avoid belts, heavy outer layers, or restrictive clothing
  • Flat or low shoes — no heels
  • Hair up if you have a neck or upper back complaint

Good to know

  • First visits are approximately 60 minutes
  • Follow-up visits are typically 20–30 minutes
  • We run on time — please arrive 10 minutes early
  • Same-day treatment is the norm, not the exception
  • We'll verify your insurance before your first visit if you call ahead
