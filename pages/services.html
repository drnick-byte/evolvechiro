Treatment Methods

How we treat.

We use a combination of evidence-based techniques tailored to each patient's specific findings and goals.

Chiropractic Adjustment

Specific spinal and extremity manipulation to restore joint mobility, reduce nerve irritation, and break the pain-spasm cycle. Applied to the spine, pelvis, and peripheral joints — shoulder, elbow, wrist, hip, knee, ankle.

Spinal Decompression

Non-surgical mechanical traction for disc-related conditions — herniated discs, bulging discs, degenerative disc disease, and disc-related sciatica. Creates negative intradiscal pressure to allow disc retraction and nerve decompression.

Soft Tissue Therapy

Manual and instrument-assisted techniques targeting muscles, tendons, and fascia. Includes active release methods, trigger point therapy, myofascial release, and Graston-style instrument-assisted work.

Webster Technique

ICPA-certified prenatal chiropractic protocol addressing sacral alignment and round ligament tension. Supports pelvic balance and optimal fetal positioning throughout pregnancy.

Corrective Exercise

Home exercise programming built around your specific findings — not generic protocols. Prescribed through Wibbi for clear visual instruction. Focused on reinforcing structural corrections and preventing recurrence.

Postural & Ergonomic Counseling

Practical guidance on workstation setup, sleeping positions, movement habits, and daily activities that directly impact your condition. Addresses the mechanical inputs that created the problem.

Digital X-Ray

On-site digital radiography when clinically indicated. Includes full spinal series — lumbopelvic, cervical, thoracic — as well as extremity imaging. Reviewed with you at the same visit.

Sports Rehabilitation

Sport-specific rehabilitation protocols for injured athletes. Progressive loading from tissue healing through return-to-sport clearance. Designed around your actual sport demands and competitive timeline.
